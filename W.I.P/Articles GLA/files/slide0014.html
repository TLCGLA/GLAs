Where Indefinite Articles DO NOT Go
•Do not use “a” or “an” before a pronoun (a word that replaces a noun).
•Incorrect: I’ve been looking for a him.
•Correct: I’ve been looking for him.
•
•Do not use “a” or “an” before an adjective without a noun after it.
•Incorrect: Nancy prefers the dress in a green.
•Correct: Nancy prefers the dress in green.
