Here Are Some Common Phrases that Use “a” or “an.”
1.Once a day / a week / a month / a year
2.Two (or any number over one) times a day / a week
3.An hour after midnight / noon / sunrise
4.Half an hour earlier / later
5.A pair of socks / a couple of times / a number of items
•
•Example: 
•Once a month, I buy a new pair of socks and a number of household cleaning items.
