Other Definite Article Rules
•Use “the” before musical instruments when discussing the instrument in general.
•I studied the piano for five years before I fell in love with the drums.
•Jae thinks the tuba is the most difficult instrument to play in marching band because it is so heavy.
•
•Use “the” when talking about a type of animal.
•Today’s dogs are descendants of the wolf.
•The housefly lives only two weeks.
•
•Use “the” when talking about a group of people.
•After graduating college, Sam wanted to help the homeless.
•Lisa was surprised to see just how much the young use their cell phones.
