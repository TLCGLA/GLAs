•In the previous slide, we were able to guess what “novice” meant.  We used context to help us guess.
•
•“Context” means “the surrounding words and ideas.”  This means we use the words and ideas around an unfamiliar word to figure out the word’s meaning.
•
•When you come across a new vocabulary word, do not reach for the dictionary right away.  Instead, try to make a logical guess based on the context of the new word.
