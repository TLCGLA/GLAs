Analyzing Tasks
•In the previous slides, we worked on breaking a major project into smaller tasks.
•However, major projects aren’t the only kinds of tasks you will have to complete.
•College students will face a wide variety of tasks, including homework, work, chores, family obligations, etc.
•You need to develop a system to decide what tasks to complete in what order.
•One system is determining how important tasks are and how urgent tasks are.
