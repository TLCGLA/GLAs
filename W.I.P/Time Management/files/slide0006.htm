Why is time management important for college students?
•College has a much less rigid schedule than high school; first time college students must adapt to increased responsibility in managing their time.
•According to the University at Buffalo’s counseling office, about 25% of students become chronic procrastinators.
•It almost always takes twice as long to complete a task as what we originally thought it would take.  This means chronic procrastinators will often run out of time or complete tasks poorly.
•Poor time management leads to lower academic performance and increased risk of dropping out.
