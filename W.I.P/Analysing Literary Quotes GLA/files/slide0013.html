Responding to a Literary Quote:
What is your reaction?
´Consider your response to literary quotes.  It may help you determine what you’d like to write about in your paper.  The response may also guide you through your analysis of the quote.
´Consider the following questions:
´What is your reaction to the quote?
´Do you have an emotional response?
´Does the quote shock or surprise you?
´Does it remind you of a situation you experienced before?  Or something you learned in another class?
´Write your response without worrying about being correct.  You can use the response to help you generate ideas, but don’t copy it directly into the final draft of an essay.
