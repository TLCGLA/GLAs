Know Your Purpose
uStrong readers know why they are reading and what they want to get out of the reading assignment.  As you read, consider the questions:
uHow might this reading relate to choice of career?
uHow does this reading relate to your class?
uWill you be tested on this material?  Asked to write an essay?  Asked to discuss it in class?
u
uIf strong readers are asked to prepare a reading for discussion, they may look for the controversial aspects of the reading and consider discussion questions in advance.  In contrast, if strong readers are asked to prepare a reading for a quiz, they may spend more time trying to memorize terms and anticipate possible quiz questions.  They can alter their strategies based on their purpose.
