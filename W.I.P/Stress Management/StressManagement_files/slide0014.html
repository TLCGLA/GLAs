Review  Worksheet
•We can apply some of the strategies we learned today about stress management on ourselves.
• Review Worksheet:  Recall a recent stressful event. What were the stress triggers? How did you react to the stress on an emotional, behavioral and physical level?  What was your perspective on the situation?
• Now lets come up with new strategies to address the stressful event. How can we reframe the problem? What  are new coping strategies that can be used? What can you do to prevent or reduce the stress?  Pair up with a student to share your responses.
