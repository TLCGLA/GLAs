Characteristics of Strong Outlines
1.Parallelism:  Use parallel structure.  If the first heading is a verb, all other headings must be verbs. 2.Coordination:  Information at the same level of the outline should have the same level of importance.  The information in Roman Numeral I should be just as important as the information in Roman Numeral II. 3.Subordination:  The information in the headings should be more general than the information in the subheadings.
4.Division:  Each heading should be divided into 2 or more parts.
