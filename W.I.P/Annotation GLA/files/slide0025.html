What should I include in my outline?
uThink of  your outline as a study guide for the chapter or reading that you’re studying.  You should include key elements from the chapter that will help you understand the meaning better, including:
uVocabulary words and their definitions
uDates and events
uPeople and what they are known for
uProcesses
uFormulas
uAny other material that is relevant to the course or upcoming assignments
