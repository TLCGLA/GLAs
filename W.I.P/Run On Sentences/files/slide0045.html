Run-on
Benjamin Franklin wanted to learn if lightning was an electrical phenomenon, he flew a kite in a thunderstorm.
Fixes
#1. A period.
#2. A semicolon.
#3. A semicolon and a transition (followed by a comma).
#4. A comma and a coordinating conjunction (FANBOY).
#5. A subordinate conjunction.
