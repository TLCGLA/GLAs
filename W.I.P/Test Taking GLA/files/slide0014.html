During The Test...
Avoid “reading into the question.”                     Don’t add anything that isn’t really there!
Resist the impulse to change an answer unless you’re SURE you made a mistake.
Use all the time you have.  Check your answer sheet for any blank spaces and make sure you erased cleanly.
Wise Advice
(Center for Academic Success; Dunham, 2004; Nugent & Vitale, 2004; UNC School of Nursing)
