Don’t Copy the Original Text
¨Don’t copy large amounts of the original text, even if you cite it properly.  (You can include brief quotes in a summary.)
¨
¨Remember, a summary should be written in your own way.  Use your words and sentences to explain the original text.
¨
¨Your professor wants to see that you understand what the text was about.  The best way to show that you understand is to write the summary in your own way.
¨
