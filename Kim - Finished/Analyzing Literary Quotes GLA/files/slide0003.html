Purpose
´When writing a literary analysis paper, quotes from the literary text become a key element of your evidence. ´Without quoting the literature, you will not be able to prove your point convincingly. ´Furthermore, analyzing literary quotes well will help you learn how to read carefully and think critically.  Both of these skills are important in the working world.  Just imagine signing a contract without reading it closely and thinking about each of the requirements critically—you could get yourself in a lot of trouble!
