•The English language often borrows words from other languages.  If you speak another language, you can use it to help you decipher English vocabulary words.
•
•Castigate = to punish
• In Spanish, “castigar” means “to punish”
•Diaphanous = sheer, transparent
• In Greek, “diafanís” (διαφανής) means “transparent”
•Carat = a unit of weight used for measuring gems
• In Arabic, “qirat” (قيراط) is the weight of four grains
