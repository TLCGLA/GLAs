TIPS FOR TAKING NOTES:
DURING THE LECTURE
•Write down the title of the lecture, the name of the course, and the date
•Pay attention to the class agenda/outline
•Don’t try to write down every word—use phrases rather than sentences and abbreviate whenever you can
•Pay attention to the summary at the end of the lecture
•At the end of the lecture, ask questions to clarify any points you don’t understand
