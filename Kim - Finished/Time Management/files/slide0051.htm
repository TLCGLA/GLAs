Avoiding Procrastination
8.Overcome fear.  We often procrastinate because we’re afraid of failing.  Face your fears and work on the task anyway (even if it is scary).
9.Get tough.  Force yourself to focus on the tasks that are high priorities, even if they aren’t fun.
10.Reward yourself.  Give yourself reasonable rewards (like going to the gym or texting your best friend) for completing tasks.    Celebrate your accomplishments to help you build up motivation for future projects.
