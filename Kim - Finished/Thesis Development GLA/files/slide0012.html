RELATING YOUR CLAIM TO YOUR SUBTOPICS
Depending on the nature of the writing assignment, you may be asked to include specific subtopics in your thesis statement.
Subtopics are smaller topics within your overall focus.  If you are writing a thesis statement with subtopics, most of the time limiting them to three works best.  However, including subtopics does not mean you do not need a claim. It must be clear to the reader how your subtopics are related.
