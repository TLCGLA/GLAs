Here Are Some Common Idioms (Sayings) that Use “a” or “an.”
1.Sound like a broken record:  to repeat the same thing over and over
2.A horse of a different color:  a completely different matter.
3.A chip on one’s shoulder:  to have a bad attitude
4.Don’t judge a book by its cover:  don’t judge something by its outward appearance
5.A slap on the wrist:  a light punishment for doing something wrong
6.A piece of cake:  something very easy
7.At a glance:  to understand something almost immediately
