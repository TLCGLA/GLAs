Mode 11:  Narrative
 Tell a story in your writing.  Present a series of events in order to inform or entertain the reader.  Narrative can be fiction or non-fiction. 
 The narrative mode gives readers a human element.  It helps readers focus on the story of an individual, which increases the likelihood that they will care about the overall topic.
 In academic writing, you are most likely to use narrative in the introduction of a paper or in the examples that you select.
 To use narrative well, focus on being detailed and interesting to capture your readers’ attention.
