Mode 1:  Definition
 To define, you explain what a word means.
 You don’t have to use a dictionary to craft a definition.  Instead, you can use this structure:
 Term= type + distinguishing characteristic
 Term:  The word you’re defining
 Type:  The category the word belongs to
 Distinguishing Characteristic:  What makes the word unique in its category
 Example:  A lion is a big cat known for its flowing mane.
