Sample Exemplification
 For some families, it is wealth, not education, that shows they have achieved the American Dream.  Luis, a first generation immigrant and first generation college student, studied tirelessly to earn his degree in English.  In his years as a college student, he didn’t earn much money while working as a tutor on campus.  His parents had hoped that Luis would work for a contractor or mechanic, working his way up in the business.  Instead, he continued his studies. 
 After completing college, though, Luis experienced a problem:  he couldn’t find a job.  He and his parents began to believe that his college education was not worthwhile.  It was only years later, when he had a job with a good salary, that Luis’s parents began to appreciate his education.
