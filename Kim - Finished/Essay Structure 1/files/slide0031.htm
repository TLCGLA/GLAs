What can a body paragraph include?
 A body paragraph may include some (or all) of the below elements.  Your paragraph may or may not need these elements depending on the type of assignment you’re working on or the subject you’re studying.  If you have questions, be sure to ask your instructor or a TLC tutor for help.
 
•Topic Sentence that connects to the thesis and reflects the subject of the entire paragraph.
•Topic sentence development:  additional details or description about the topic sentence.
•Evidence:  quotes, statistics, examples, illustrations, etc. from reputable sources.
•Commentary on the Evidence:  show readers how the evidence supports your argument.
•Connections back to the thesis statement:  show readers how the entire paragraph supports your argument.
•Transitional words or phrases:  help readers make connections between your ideas.
