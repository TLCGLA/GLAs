Formatting your paper in APA style means paying attention to mechanical details such as:
•Typeface
•Line spacing
•margins
•Page headers

•Double space the entire paper
•Also double space between the text and titles, headings and block quotations
•Double space the reference list and figure captions
•Indent the first line of every paragraph ½ inch
•Align the text to the left hand margin, leaving a ragged right-hand margin
•Page numbers consecutively starting with page 1
