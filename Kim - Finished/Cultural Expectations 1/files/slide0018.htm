Communicating with Your Professor
•Your professors will expect you to communicate with them, especially if you are having trouble in the class.  Be active in your own education by communicating with professors.
•Usually, your professor will let you know what to call him or her.  Don’t be surprised if a professor asks you to use his/her first name.  If the professor doesn’t tell you what to call him/her, use the title Professor and his/her last name.
•Find out which communication channel your professor prefers.  Some professors prefer to use email, and others like to meet with students in person.
•Visiting a professor’s office hours is a wonderful way to get to know your professor.  Professors reserve time (called “office hours”) to meet with their students outside of the classroom.  You can get answers to your specific questions or advice on your assignments.  Knowing your professor on a one-on-one basis can help in the future if you need to ask for a letter of recommendation.
