Finding Good Team Members
•One way to improve your chances of success is to find good team members to work with.  Consider the following:
•Find a diverse group (different sexes, ethnicities, and ages) because each member will bring his/her own perspective to the team.
•Find team members who can do things that you can’t.  This will allow you to broaden your skill set.  You can also teach them the skills that you know.
•Find one responsible person to be the team leader (this could be you!).  That person should make sure that the team is on track to completing the assignment successfully.
•Avoid working with your close friends.  Sometimes, working with friends can be distracting.  Instead, work with people you get along with that will stay focused on the assignment.
