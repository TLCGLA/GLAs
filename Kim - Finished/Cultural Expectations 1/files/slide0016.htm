Class Discussions
•Although professors may lead a class discussion, they will usually not dominate the discussion.
•They will expect students to participate by expressing their opinions and responding to their classmates.
•Throughout this process, it is important to be respectful to all the students.  You do not have to agree with your classmates, but you do need to reply to them politely.
•To prepare for a class discussion, be sure to read any assigned readings.  Come to class with questions or important points that you want to discuss.
•For many instructors, discussions are an important part of class participation.  In order to get a good grade, you should be prepared to contribute to the class discussion.  Instructors have different ways of grading your participation.  Be sure to ask your professors how you can earn credit for participating.
