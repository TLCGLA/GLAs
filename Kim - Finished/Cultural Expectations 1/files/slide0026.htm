Opportunities to Participate
•Class activities are done in pairs, in groups, or alone, so you ought to be confident while sharing your views.
•In a full-class discussion, be sure to raise your hand to indicate that you want to speak.
•When you work with a study group, you have the opportunity to discuss the material with your peers and prepare for exams.  This can be a very helpful strategy.
•The Learning Center (TLC) provides many opportunities for working on team projects, such as study rooms and tutors.
