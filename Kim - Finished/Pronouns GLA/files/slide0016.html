Possessive Pronouns
•What do you own? Do you own any clothes? If yes, the clothes are yours.
•
•Samples:
•I live in a large house. I bought this house when I moved to California in 2000. The house is mine.
•You love to drive luxury cars. That new red Ferrari is yours.
•We all live on this earth. The earth is ours. Let’s preserve it.
