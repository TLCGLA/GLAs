Relative Pronouns:  Who vs. Whom
This is the man whom I told you about. 
Whom is in the object position.  One way to know this for sure is to rewrite the sentence as, I told you about him.  Since him is an object pronoun, I know I must use whom here.

He is the only person I know who can run two marathons in a month.
Who is in the subject position.  One way to know this for sure is to rewrite the sentence as, He can run two marathons in a month.  Since he is a subject pronoun, I know I must use who here.
