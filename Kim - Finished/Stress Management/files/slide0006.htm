Stress Facts
•In 2014, the American Psychological Association (APA) conducted a survey called “Stress in America:  Paying with Our Health.”  They found that:
•On average, Americans rate their stress at 4.9 on a 10 point scale (1=little to no stress and 10=a great deal of stress)
•42% of adults say they are not doing enough—or are not sure whether they are doing enough—to manage their stress
•Common sources of stress are money (64%), work (60%), the economy (49%), family responsibilities (47%), and personal health concerns (46%).
