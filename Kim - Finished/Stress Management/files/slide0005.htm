What is Stress?
•Stress is the body’s reaction to the different challenges we face in life.
•It developed through evolution as a way to protect our ancestors from predators and other threats.
•Some stress is beneficial; it helps us get things done.
•Too much stress can be damaging to our health.  According to the American Psychological Association, “sudden emotional stresses…can trigger heart attacks, arrhythmias, and even sudden death.”
•When stress becomes chronic (interfering with your life for an extended period of time), its effects can be even more damaging.
