Strategy #4:  Exercise
•Exercise releases hormones like endorphins which block pain and create a feeling of euphoria.  In other words, exercise can help you blow off steam!
•As little as 20 minutes of exercise a day can decrease stress levels.
•Find a type of exercise you enjoy, like yoga, hiking, dancing, or playing team sports.
•Exercise can also help you sleep better at night.
•Note:  Check with your doctor before altering your exercise routine.
