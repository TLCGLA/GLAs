Counseling Department
•The Counseling Department provides educational, career, and personal counseling and program advisement.
•It aims to empower students by helping them establish goals, evaluate options, develop an educational plan, and learn to study effectively.
•Students can meet with counseling faculty privately to discuss personal and academic goals and challenges.
