Ask the question, “so what?”
 After making a statement in your paper, consider the question, “so what?”  This question asks you to consider the significance of your topic.  In other words, why should readers consider the topic important? 
 By answering the question, “so what?” you can engage your readers and (hopefully) encourage them to adopt your ideas.
 The question “so what?” is an extremely valuable tool to help you succeed in your college classes.  Keep asking “so what?” to push yourself further.  You’ll come up with more complex, interesting ideas.
