Let the research lead your ideas
•Key Concept:  Research first, then develop your argument.
•In order to develop strong ideas, we must do research first.
•That way, we know that the information (well-researched data) will support our ideas.  Be sure to research all sides of the issue (not just the side you agree with).
•Avoid the frustration of looking for “the perfect quote” for your paper.
•Instead, learn your topic well and build your ideas based on the data (not the other way around).
