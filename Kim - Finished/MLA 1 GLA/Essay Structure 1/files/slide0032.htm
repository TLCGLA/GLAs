Transitional Words and Phrases
 Transitions are words and phrases that show how two ideas are connected.  Using transitions will help readers follow your ideas better.
 You can use transitions within or between paragraphs.  Be careful not to over-use transitions.  You don’t need one in every sentence.
 Some common transitions include:
•In addition
•For example
•Although
•In fact
