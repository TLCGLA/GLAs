What goes in an introduction?
 Typically, academic essay introductions include:
 1.  Attention Grabber (optional)
 2.  Background Information
 3.  Thesis
 Of course, this may change based on assignment and the subject you’re studying.  (Scientific lab reports, for example, won’t include an attention grabber.)  If you have questions about your particular assignment, talk to your instructor or to a TLC tutor.
