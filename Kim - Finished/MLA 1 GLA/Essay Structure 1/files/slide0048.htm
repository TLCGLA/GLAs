Assignment Requirements
 When working on your paper, review the assignment requirements frequently.  Use them to help make sure that your paper is structured effectively.
 Examples:
If you are writing a 10 page paper, you should know that you need more than five paragraphs.
If you are writing a compare/contrast paper, you should know that you need to discuss at least two main subjects.
If you are writing a formal lab report for your chemistry class, you should know that your language should be clear, specific, and scientifically appropriate.
 Your assignment will give you clues as to how to approach the writing.  If you need help interpreting those clues, talk to your instructor or a TLC tutor.
