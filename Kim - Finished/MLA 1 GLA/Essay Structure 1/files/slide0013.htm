Why write an introduction?
 The introduction of an essay introduces readers to the ideas you’re going to share.  Readers need some background information from you to help them understand your topic.
 The introduction fulfills several purposes:
•Identifying your topic
•Providing background information
•Encouraging readers to be interested in your topic
•Sharing your argument about the topic
•Providing readers an idea of who you are as a writer
◦
 Note:  You don’t have to write the introduction first.  Sometimes, it works better to write the body of the essay first and then go back to the introduction.
