Lost Momentum
–You may have started your paper, but find yourself running out of things to say. 
–
–
–
–
–
–
–
Solutions:
1.Give yourself a break.  Get away from your paper for a while so you can return to it with fresh eyes.
2.Talk to someone else (the instructor, a tutor, a friend) about your topic. You may come up with new ideas while youre talking.
3.In a different document, try writing in a more informal style (like a blog post).  This can help you generate ideas without worrying about whether or not they sound academic.
4.Play Devils Advocate.  Pretend that you are arguing the other side of the issue.  By looking at the issue from a new angle, you may develop more material to write about.
