Anecdote
•Anecdote:  A short personal story with a point.
•Example:
• At eighteen, I moved out of my parents’ house, got a job, and began the project of being an independent adult—or so I thought.  I believed that having and using a credit card was the thing adults did.  I believed that a credit card would let me live a happy, comfortable life with all the stuff I could ever want.  I believed that credit was extra income, just like my paycheck.  I was wrong.
