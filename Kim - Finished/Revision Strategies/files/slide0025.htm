Revision Strategy:  Reverse Outline
1.Number your body paragraphs.
2.Rewrite each of your topic sentences on a new piece of paper using the numbers of your body paragraphs (you can also use index cards or sticky notes).
3.Reorganize the topic sentences; try several organizational strategies.
4.When you are happy with your new organization, reorder your body paragraphs to match the new order.
5.Review your thesis.  You may have to revise the thesis based on your new organization.
