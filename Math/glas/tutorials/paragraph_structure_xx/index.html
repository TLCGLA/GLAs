Paragraph Structure (Writing Source-Based Body Paragraphs)
Introduction

When writing essays, you often have to cite sources in the body of the essay. In this guided learning activity, you will learn the correct way to incorporate sources in paragraphs.

Student Learning Outcomes

By the end of this activity, you should be able to:

  • Compose a source based paragraph of 8- 11 sentences
  • Integrate 2-3 direct quotations in a single paragraph
  • Effectively contextualize and analyze the quotations
