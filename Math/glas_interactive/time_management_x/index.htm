Time Management

Introduction

Welcome to your time management guided learning activity. Time management is an important skill for you to master early on in your college education. It takes practice and a strong ability to efficiently prioritize activities. Students are often surprised by how much time they spend on various activities. You will be guided through the basics of time management and complete several exercises that will help you put into practice what you learn. You will evaluate your current time management skills and learn ways to make time for all the important activities in your life.

Student Learning Outcomes

By the end of this activity, you should be able to:
  • Evaluate and create a student schedule
  • Indicate three ways to use time more effectively
