Geometry Problems Quiz
1.
An angle is equal to 20 degrees more than four times its complement. Find the measure of the angle and the measure of the complement.
2.
A triangle with two equal angles is called isosceles. Find all of the angles of an isosceles triangle if the third angle is 30 degrees more than the measure of one of the equal angles.
3.
The length of a rectangle is 14 mm longer than five times the width. Find the length and width of the rectangle if the perimeter is 148 mm.
4.
The area of a trapezoid is 75 square inches. If the height is 10 inches and one of the bases is 3 inches. Find the length of the other base.
