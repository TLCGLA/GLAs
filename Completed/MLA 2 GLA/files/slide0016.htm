¡If the poem is divided by part, stanza, and line numbers, list them and put periods between each section.
¡
¡Example:
¡Homer’s Iliad begins with a description of Achilles’s rage, characterizing it as “murderous anger which condemned Achaeans / to countless agonies” (1.1.2-3).
¡
¡Note that…
¡There is a slash mark after “Achaeans” to show where the line would break
¡The first 1 in the citation refers to the part; the quote comes from the first book of the Iliad
¡The second 1 in the citation refers to the stanza
¡The 2-3 refers to the line numbers in the poem
