THE THESIS STATEMENT IS THE GUIDE FOR YOUR ESSAY
A strong thesis will guide you to writing the information necessary to proving your point.  All information in the essay should contribute to proving the thesis.  If the information does not help support the thesis, it should be removed from the essay.
As you work your way through a draft, refer back to the thesis frequently.  Ask yourself:
1.What does my reader need to learn to accept my thesis statement?
2.What is the best order for presenting my information?
