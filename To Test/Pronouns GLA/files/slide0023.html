Reflexive Pronouns: Correcting Common Mistakes
• “Hisself” is a common error.  Correct it by writing “himself.”
•Corrected:  A man injured himself while trying to fix a TV antenna.
•
•“Ourself” and “themself,” are mistakes because they are trying to be singular when they must be plural.  Correct them by writing “ourselves” or “themselves.”
•Corrected:
•We decided among ourselves to form a study group.
•My neighbors mostly live by themselves. They rarely have guests.
