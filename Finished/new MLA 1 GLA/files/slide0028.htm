1.  AUTHOR.
•The author is the person primarily responsible for producing the work.
•Begin your citation with the author’s name.
•Use last name, first name (for the first author only)
•Two authors
•Follow the same order that is used in the published work
•Example:  Dorris, Michael, and Louise Erdrich.
•Three or more authors
•Use the last name, first name for the author listed first
•Then, add “et al.” (meaning “and others”)
•Example:  Burdick, Anne, et al.
•Pseudonyms and User Names
•Use the name the way it is published
•Example:  @nerdist
•No Author name:  skip directly to the work’s title
