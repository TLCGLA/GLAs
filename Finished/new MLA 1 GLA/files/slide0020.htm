POSSIBLE SIGNAL PHRASES
•You can use a wide variety of signal phrases.  The most common is probably “according to,” but you can also use any verb that means “says.”
•
•For example:
•David Shenk states
•David Shenk argues
•David Shenk emphasizes
•David Shenk describes
•David Shenk illustrates
•David Shenk notes
•David Shenk suggests
