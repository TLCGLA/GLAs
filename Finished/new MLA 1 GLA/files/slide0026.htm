THE PHILOSOPHY BEHIND THE 8TH EDITION
•In previous editions of MLA style, students would first determine the type of publication they were citing (book, online article, TV show, etc.), then find the correct citation style for that source.
•This system became problematic because technology moved more swiftly than MLA citation updates.  Whenever a new technology became available (like Twitter), MLA had to devise a new kind of citation.
•The 8th Edition corrects this problem.  It provides a template of core elements that most published sources will have.  Writers can use the template for any work they plan to cite.
