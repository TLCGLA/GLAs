Running Out of Time
–When you are running out of time on an assignment, you may feel a lot of pressure to finish.  This pressure can prevent you from knowing what to write about next.
–
–
–
–
–
–
–
Solutions:
1.Give yourself plenty of time to work on an assignment.
2.Time yourself as you work on writing projects.  This will help you figure out just how long it takes for you to write a paper.
3.Start working on your assignment as soon as you receive instructions, even if you only have time to jot down a few notes or think through your ideas.  Giving your brain time to think through the essay will make it easier to write.
