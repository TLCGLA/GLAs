Cubing Process:
Six Sides of the Cube
1.Describe:  How would you describe your issue or topic?
2.Compare/Contrast:  What is your topic similar to or different from?
3.Analyze:  How would you break the topic into smaller parts?
4.Apply:  How can this information be used in other situations?  Or, can you find examples to illustrate your topic?
5.Significance:  Why is this topic important?
6.Argue:  What is your position on this topic?
