New Type of Writing
–Students may experience writer’s block when doing a new type of writing, such as the first time they write a research paper. 
Solutions:
1.Review the assignment prompt carefully
2.Look at examples of other texts in this genre (for example, look at other research papers as you prepare to write your research paper)
3.Find out what elements you need to include (Introduction?  Quotes?  Images?)
4.Ask your professor for help
5.Ask a TLC tutor for help
