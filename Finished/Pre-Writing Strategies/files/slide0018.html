But won’t I run out of things to write?
–Nope!  Students are more likely to run out of things to write if their topic is too broad.  Often times, with a broad topic, students won’t know where they can go into detail, so they don’t go into detail anywhere.  The papers can become vague, which will usually not convince readers to agree with the students’ ideas.
–
–With a narrow topic, you have space to really dig in to the material.  You can include high quality research and, depending on the assignment, really interesting examples.
