Cubing Sample
–Topic:  Texting while Driving
–*Note:  This is an abbreviated sample
1.Describe:  When drivers text, their eyes leave the road and they become distracted.  They are a danger to themselves, their passengers, pedestrians, and fellow drivers.
2.Compare:  Texting while driving can be compared to drunk driving.
3.Analyze:  I could write about the compulsion to text, the effects of texting while driving, the way the media discusses the issue, or strategies people use to prevent themselves from texting.
4.Apply:  I should look up famous cases of texting while driving.  Have any of them gone to court?
5.Significance:  The issue is important because texting while driving presents a significant danger to people travelling the roads.  As a society, we should strive to have safe roads and responsible drivers.
6.Argue:  I argue that fines for texting while driving should be increased.  In addition, people caught texting while driving should be required to take a special traffic school course.
