Other Grade Possibilities
Grade
Meaning
I
Incomplete: A student experiences an emergency in the last week of class that prevents him/her from finishing the course
W
Withdrawal: The student withdraws from the class after the official drop date. A grade of W will affect the number of times the student is allowed to take the course.
P
Pass
N
No Pass
CR
Credit
NC
No Credit
RD
Report Delayed: The professor is unable to report your grade by the time grades are due.
