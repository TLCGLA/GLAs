THESIS STATEMENT CRITERIA:
A strong thesis statement accomplishes the following:
•It summarizes the whole essay in one sentence
The thesis should provide a preview for the essay.  Let readers know what they will read about.
•It predicts, controls, and obligates
The thesis is a guide.  Use it to help you construct a logical argument.
•It makes a unified claim that is debatable (readers may disagree) or that needs to be proved (readers need convincing).
The thesis should have some controversy.  If all readers agree about a topic, there is little point in writing about it.
•It should be narrow and specific.
The thesis should provide a clear picture of the point you plan to make in an essay.
