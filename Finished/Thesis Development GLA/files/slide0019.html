A SAMPLE THESIS STRUCTURE:
ALTHOUGH…BECAUSE
One way to structure a thesis is to use an “Although…because” structure. 
•Begin with “Although,” which is a counterargument for your topic
•Then, state your claim
•Finally, add “because” to explain your reasons
Sample Thesis:
Counterargument
Although cell phone users enjoy upgrading their phones to access new features,
Claim
they should avoid upgrading frequently
Reasons
because the discarded phone creates waste and manufacturing new phones strains our natural resources.
