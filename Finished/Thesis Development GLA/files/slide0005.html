HOW TO GENERATE A THESIS STATEMENT IF THE TOPIC IS ASSIGNED
Step 1:  Turn the assignment into a question.  For example:
Assignment:  Write an essay on the effects of cell phone use on modern society.
Question:  How have cell phones affected modern society?
Step 2:  Compose one or two sentences answering that question.  For example:
Answer:  Face-to-face interaction has been replaced by interaction with phones, making social relations more impersonal.
