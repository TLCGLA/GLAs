WHEN ACTIVELY LISTENING, USE THE FOLLOWING CUES TO RECOGNIZE WHEN THE SPEAKER IS MAKING AN IMPORTANT POINT:
•Pausing
•Giving Examples
•Repeating him/herself
•Repeating the Textbook
•Increasing volume/changing pitch
•Taking more time in one area
•Adding activities or worksheets
•Using body language
•Writing on the board
•Using direct statements (this is important) or signal words (significant, most)
