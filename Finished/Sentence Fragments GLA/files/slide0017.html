Examples of Relative Clause Fragments
Relative Clause Fragments
Corrected by Adding a Main Clause
Julia has an old car.  Which is giving her trouble.
Julia has an old car, which is giving her trouble.
I want to meet the woman.  Who  saved my son from drowning.
I want to meet the woman who saved my son from drowning.
Will admires the car.  That gets excellent gas mileage.
Will admires the car that gets excellent gas mileage.
