Examples of Infinitive Phrase Fragments
Infinitive Phrase Fragments
Corrected by Adding an Independent Clause
To be an astronaut.
To be an astronaut, you need a great deal of scientific knowledge.
To impress his future in-laws.
He made a gourmet meal to impress his future in-laws.
To climb Mount Everest.
To climb Mount Everest, we must prepare carefully for years.
