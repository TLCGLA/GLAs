Examples of Subordinate Clause Fragments
Subordinate Clause Fragments
Corrected by Adding a Main Clause
After the rain stopped.
After the rain stopped, John went out to play.
Because it is a  holiday.
Many people are traveling because it is a holiday.
Unless Noah finishes his homework.
Unless Noah finishes his homework, he won’t be allowed to play baseball.
If I can’t complete this activity today.
If I can’t complete this activity today, I will finish it tomorrow.
