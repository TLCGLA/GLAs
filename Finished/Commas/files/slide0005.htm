WHEN TO USE A COMMA IN A LIST
•Use a comma when listing three or more things in a series.
•Examples:
•Maddie made a quick to-do list in her head.  She has to go to school, print out her paper, and read a chapter in the next fifteen minutes!
•Jerry just signed his first record deal.  He’s already thinking about his future projects: touring Asia with his band, making a multimillion dollar music video, and performing a duet with Taylor Swift at the Grammys.
