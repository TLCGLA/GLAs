•The standard methods for completing in-text citations are covered in the MLA 1 Workshop.  However, this slide will provide a brief review.
•
•What is an in-text citation? 
•It is the way students cite quotes within the body of their paper. 
•
•What does an in-text citation include?
•For a quote from a book with page numbers, an in-text citation should include:
•A signal phrase that includes the name of the author
•The page number where the quote can be found
