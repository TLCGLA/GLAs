Specific Test-Taking Techniques
•  Read the directions carefully.  Will you answer some questions or all?  Are some questions worth more points?  Do only what you are asked to do!
•  Quickly read each question.  Make brief notes with key words and ideas that come to mind.
•  Figure the time you can spend on each question (more time for questions with higher point values).  Save 10-15 minutes for proofreading and review.
With essay questions, get a good start!
