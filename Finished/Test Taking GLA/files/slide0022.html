Specific Test-Taking Techniques
•  Restate the question in your own words, then reread the question.  Make sure you know what is asked!
•  Briefly outline or plan each answer.  Be sure to include an introduction and a summary to make your essay more complete and polished.
•  After you’ve answered each question, go back to proofread and review.  Correct any spelling or grammar errors.
With essay questions, you create the product!
