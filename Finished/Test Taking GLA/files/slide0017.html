Specific Test-Taking Techniques
•  Identify key words in the stem that indicate negative polarity (e.g., not, except, never, least, unexpected, unrelated)
•  Identify key words in the stem that set a priority (e.g., first, initially, best, most)
•  Answer the question mentally before looking at the possible answers.
With multiple choice questions,                       look at the stem for important information!
(Center for Academic Success; Dunham, 2004; Nugent & Vitale, 2004; UNC School of Nursing)
