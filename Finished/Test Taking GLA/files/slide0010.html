Before The Test...
How many tests does the course have?  How much does each one count?
What material is covered by this test?
How many questions will there be?  In what format?
What facts and concepts have been emphasized by the instructor?
What do your classmates think will be important to know for this test?
(Center for Academic Success; Dunham, 2004; Nugent & Vitale, 2004; UNC School of Nursing)
Do some detective work!
