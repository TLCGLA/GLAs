Before The Test...
Get enough rest and exercise!
As often as possible, get 6-8 hours of sleep each night.
Get a full night’s sleep before the test.
Exercise relieves stress and gives your brain oxygen!
Exercise helps you sleep better.
As often as possible, including a few hours before the test, eat healthy.
Follow your regular routine before the test.
(Center for Academic Success; Dunham, 2004; Nugent & Vitale, 2004; UNC School of Nursing)
