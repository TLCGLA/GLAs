•You can use a wide variety of signal phrases.  The most common is probably “according to,” but you can also use any verb that means “says.”
•
•For example:
•Dr. Carol Dweck states
•Dr. Carol Dweck argues
•Dr. Carol Dweck emphasizes
•Dr. Carol Dweck describes
•Dr. Carol Dweck illustrates
•Dr. Carol Dweck notes
•Dr. Carol Dweck suggests
