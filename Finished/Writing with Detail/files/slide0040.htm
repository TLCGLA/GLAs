Let’s look at a sample
 Idea:  Failure can be beneficial.
 “How” Questions:
◦How can failure benefit people?
◦How do people fail?
◦How do people learn from their failures?
◦How can they avoid failing in the future?
◦
◦By answering one or more of these questions, you can enhance your discussion of the idea that failure is beneficial.  Readers will have a better idea of what the idea means.
