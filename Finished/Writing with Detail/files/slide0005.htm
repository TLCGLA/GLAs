Preview Activity Discussion
 There are many different ways a teacher can be good.  In addition, students’ own opinions about teaching, teachers, and education will affect their answers.
 For example, one student may say a good teacher is one who explains things clearly while another student may say a good teacher is one who can relate the material to real life.
 When we write sentences like “I had a good teacher,” we are leaving the readers to make their own meaning.  They may interpret the sentence differently than we did. 
 Instead, we will work on adding detail to our sentences to make sure our readers understand exactly what we mean.
