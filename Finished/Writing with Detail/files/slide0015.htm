Let’s look at a sample
 You’re writing a paper about overcoming obstacles to education.  You’ve decided to write about procrastination.
 Sample description:  Procrastination occurs when people knowingly put off tasks for later that they should be completing now.  One key characteristic of procrastination is a feeling of guilt; the procrastinator understands that his/her time would be better spent doing something else.  Procrastinators may be putting off tasks for a number of reasons, including a fear of failure or disliking the tasks or the topic.
 Discussion:  In this example, the writer explained exactly what procrastination is.  The writer ensures that readers understand the definition of procrastination.  Readers can better understand the point the writer will make in the rest of the paper.
