Important Pronoun Rules!
•
•Don’t mix up subject and object pronouns –
•My brother and me went to visit our grandparent – wrong!
•My brother and I went to visit our grandparents – correct!
•
•When one of the subjects is I, place it after the other noun or pronoun.
•I and my sister went to see the zoo – wrong!
•My sister and I  went to see the zoo – correct!
