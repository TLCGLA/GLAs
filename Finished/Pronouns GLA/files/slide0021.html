Reflexive Pronouns with ‘by’
•When the preposition ‘by’ is used before a reflexive pronoun, it means “alone.”
•
•For example:  By myself, by yourself, by herself, by itself, by themselves, by ourselves,  etc.
•
•Samples:
•The girls said they could clean the room by themselves. They did not need any help.
•“I want to be by myself; I need some privacy.”
•
•“By themselves” suggests “they can do the job alone without anybody’s help.”
•“By myself” suggests “I want to be alone.”
