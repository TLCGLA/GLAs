Reading without Pronouns
•Now read this:
•Harry is a student from Australia. Harry is planning to visit Harry’s parents after ten years. Harry did not see Harry’s parents in ten years. Harry’s parents will be happy to see Harry.
•
-How did the reading sound? Funny? Odd? Why?
-It is because the same nouns are repeated over and over again.
•
•Without pronouns, the nouns will have to be repeated over and over again, making the reading sound strange.
