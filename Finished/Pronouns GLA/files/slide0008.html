Pronouns: Plural
•Plural pronouns replace plural nouns:
•Look at Mark and James. They are going to close the library now. Tina and I are working on a project, but we must leave.
•‘They’ refers to Mark and James (two people).
•‘We’ refers to Tina and I (two people).
•
•Plural pronouns: We, you, they, these, those, who, whom, etc.
