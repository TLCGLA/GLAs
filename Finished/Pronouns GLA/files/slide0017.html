Possessive Pronouns
•Let’s look at possessive pronouns in another way. Read the following:
•
•Tom opened the fridge and began to eat from a lunch bag. When I saw that, I cried, “ That is my lunch. Yours is in the other fridge!”
•
•“Yours” refers to Tom’s lunch bag.
•
•Mine is a life of constant struggle, but yours is easy-going.
•“Mine” refers to my life.
