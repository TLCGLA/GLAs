Demonstrative Pronouns
•Demonstrative pronouns replace definite or specific nouns.
•
•Now read the following and see how the meaning changes from the previous sample:
•
•These chocolate brownies are delicious. Please give me this one, too. I don’t want that one; it’s broken.
•
•In the above sentence, these,” “this,” and “that” indicate particular brownies.
•
•The demonstrative pronouns are:  this, these, that, those
•
•You probably already know:
•“This” and “these” refer to nearby things
•“That” and “those” refer to things that are further away
