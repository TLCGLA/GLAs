Indefinite Pronouns
•Indefinite pronouns replace indefinite or unspecific nouns.
•Sample:
•That chocolate brownie was delicious. Can I have another? Any will do.
•
•“Another” and “any” do not mean any particular brownie. They mean unspecific pieces of brownies.
•
•Some indefinite pronouns are: all, another, anything, nobody, nothing, something, etc.
