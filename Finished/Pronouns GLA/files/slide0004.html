Nouns
•Read the following sentences:
•
•Colleges are for students. Valencia has a good college.
•Mother made us some waffles for breakfast.
•Mr. Smith is a marine biologist. He knows diving.
•What do you see? Names of people, names of places, names of things, names of  actions.
•Well, these are all nouns. Now look at some more noun words:

People: Harry, Jane, Mr. Smith
•Places: Valencia, Los Angeles
•Things: college, waffles, phones
•Actions: skiing, swimming, reading
• Ideas: truth, beauty, democracy
