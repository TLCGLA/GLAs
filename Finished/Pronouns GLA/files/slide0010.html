Subject Pronouns
•When a noun is the subject of a sentence, replace it with a subject pronoun.
•
•Sample:
•Tesla is an American car.  Tesla runs on electricity, and Tesla was first introduced in 2008. Ever since, Tesla has gained popularity among its buyers.
•
•“Tesla” is the subject in all the sentences. It needs to be replaced by the subject pronoun “it.”
•
•Sample with Pronouns:
•Tesla is an American car. It runs on electricity, and it was first introduced in 2008. Ever since, it has gained popularity among its buyers.
•
•Some subject pronouns are: I, you, he, she, it, we, & they.
