Time Management Overview:  How Much Time Do We Have?
•Everyone has 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
•This makes a budget of 168 hours each week.
•168 hours may seem like a lot of time.  BUT…
•If you sleep 8 hours a day (56 hours a week), you have 112 hours left
•And, if you are taking 12 units (about 12 hours a week),  you have 100 hours left
•And, if you are studying sufficiently for your classes (3 hours per unit or 36 hours), you have 64 hours left.
•This leaves only 64 hours for everything else you have to do, including:
•Eating
•Going to work
•Commuting
•Exercising / Attending Athletic Practice
•Showering and Personal Grooming
