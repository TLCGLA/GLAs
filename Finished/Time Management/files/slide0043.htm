The Planning Fallacy
•Some people procrastinate because they fall into the planning fallacy, which is:
•They underestimate how long it will take to complete a task.
•They do not take into account how long it has taken to complete a similar task in the past.
•They plan for everything to go smoothly.  In other words, they don’t save any time for unforeseen circumstances (like a computer crashing or changing a flat tire).
