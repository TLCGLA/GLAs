Daily Lists
•Planning major projects takes many days.
•In addition to those projects, we have other duties to complete.
•We can keep track of those duties by making daily To Do lists.
•This will help you remember your tasks.
•In addition, you can feel a sense of satisfaction when you cross off an item.
•You can write your To Do list on paper or electronically; just be sure to write down the tasks you have to complete.
•Make your To Do list part of your daily routine.  It will help you accomplish your goals!
