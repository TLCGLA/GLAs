Urgent
•Urgent tasks are those that need to be completed quickly.
•They are often unexpected problems
•Urgent tasks cause stress and make us feel like we are in constant crisis mode.
•They can also be dictated by someone else’s agenda; for example, it might feel urgent to respond to a text message from a best friend, even if responding interferes with your agenda.
•Ideally, we should manage our time to prevent tasks from becoming urgent.
