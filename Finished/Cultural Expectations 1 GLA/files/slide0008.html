Syllabus
•
•On the first day of class, you will receive a Syllabus, which is an outline for the class.  The syllabus is a vital document that explains exactly what is expected of you over the course of the semester.
•You can expect to see the following components in most syllabi:
•Required Books
•List of Assignments
•Grading Policy
•Attendance/Drop policy
•Academic Integrity Policy
•Office Hours/Location
•Instructor’s contact information
•Disability accommodations
•Student Learning Outcomes
•Assignment/Reading Calendar (this could also be a separate handout)
