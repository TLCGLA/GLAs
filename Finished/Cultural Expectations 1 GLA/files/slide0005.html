Classrooms are Diverse
•
•American college classrooms are highly diverse in terms of age, gender, race, language, ethnicity, and culture.
•For example, at COC in 2013-2014, we had 18,514 students.
•48% Female and 52% Male
•43.4% Latino, 38.1% White, 5% Asian, and 4.8% African American
•34.2% of students are over 25
•Although this may be uncomfortable at first, you have the benefit of interacting with a wide variety of people.
