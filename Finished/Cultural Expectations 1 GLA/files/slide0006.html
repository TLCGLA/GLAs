Classes Are Uniform in Learning Levels
•Although your class is diverse in terms of age, gender, race, etc., the students are at very similar learning levels.
•They are placed in the class either through placement tests or performance in previous classes.
•Since you are all at the same level, the professor can meet the students’ needs.
•The students can work as a team to understand the material better.
•Note:  Many disciplines, like ESL or math, have multiple levels for their classes.  Be sure to talk to your counselor to find out what your next class will be.
