Analyzing a Literary Quote:
What does it mean?
´Analysis involves looking closely at a literary quote to figure out what that quote means.  This requires students to go beyond summarizing and responding to quotes (although a response is usually a good first step to an analysis).
´When analyzing, you can examine:
´Diction (word choice)
´Imagery
´Symbolism
´Character(s), including the narrator
´Tone
´Other literary devices (simile, metaphor, alliteration, etc.)
´Any assumptions made in the text
´The implications of the text’s (or character’s) assertions
´The use of grammar or syntax
