Relevance
´Be sure to select a quote that is relevant to the point you’re trying to prove.
´
´Example:  You are writing a paragraph that argues that Miss Emily Grierson of “A Rose for Emily” is a victim of her father’s patriarchal control.
´Irrelevant:  “Already we knew that there was one room in that region above stairs which no one had seen in forty years, and which would have to be forced” (Faulkner 1381).
´Relevant:  “We had long thought of them as a tableau, Miss Emily a slender figure in white in the background, her father a spraddled silhouette in the foreground, his back to her and clutching a horsewhip” (Faulkner 1378).
