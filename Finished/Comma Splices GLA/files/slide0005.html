Dependent Clauses
•Now read these:
•Because John was sick yesterday.
•After he got home.
•Before he went to bed.
•Understand this:
•Do the sentences above make sense?
• Because John was sick yesterday – (what happened ?)
•After he got home – (then what happened?)
•Before he went to bed – (what happened ?)
•
•These sentences do not include a complete thought.  Therefore, they cannot stand alone.
•They are not independent clauses. They are dependent clauses.
