Independent & Dependent Clauses
•Example:
•Yesterday, John came to class without doing his homework because he was sick. He rarely misses a homework assignment. Yesterday, after he got home, he felt sick to his stomach. He took some medicine before he went to bed.
•
•Understand this:
•Some additional information is necessary to make an incomplete thought complete.
•This additional information comes in the form of independent clauses.
•Each dependent clause must connect to an independent clause. It cannot function alone.
