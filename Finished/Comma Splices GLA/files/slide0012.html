Correct a Comma Splice with a Period (.)
•College of the Canyons offers many interesting courses. These courses attract students from near and far.
•We have created two sentences from the original comma splice.
•The comma splice is corrected!
•
•Let’s take another example:
•People graduate from college with high hopes, they try to get good jobs after graduation.
•Correct this comma splice by removing the comma and separating the independent clauses by a period.
•
•Correction:
•People graduate from college with high hopes.  They try to get good jobs after graduation.
