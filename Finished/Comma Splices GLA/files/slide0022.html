Correct with Conjunctive Adverbs
•Let’s look at the same example again:
•My sister makes delicious mushroom omelets, she hates eating eggs and mushrooms.
•
•We can also correct the comma splice by making two separate sentences.
•
•Corrected:
•My sister makes delicious mushroom omelets.  However, she hates eating eggs and mushrooms.
•You can begin the second sentence with the conjunctive adverb “however.”  Notice that you must follow “however” with a comma.
