Correct with Conjunctive Adverbs
•Example:
•My sister makes delicious mushroom omelets, she hates eating eggs and mushrooms.
•These are independent clauses that are joined by a comma. Therefore, the example is a comma splice.
•
•Corrected:
•My sister makes delicious mushroom omelets; however, she hates eating eggs and mushrooms.
•To correct the sentence, add the conjunctive adverb “however.”  Notice that you must also add a semicolon before “however” and a comma after it.
