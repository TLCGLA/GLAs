What Is a Definite Article?
•The definite article is “the.”  It shows that the noun refers to a specific member of a group.
•
•Let’s look at an example:
•The house is decorated with lights.
•The sentence refers to a specific house.  It does not apply to any house, but to one particular house.
•
•Here’s another example:
•Aziz was afraid to disturb the bee hive.
•The sentence refers to one particular bee hive.
