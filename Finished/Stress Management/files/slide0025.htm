Self-sabotage
•Overall, unhealthy stress reduction techniques have us engage in self-sabotage.  In other words, we do not act in our own best interest.  In fact, they may cause more problems in the long run.
•These techniques include:
•Drinking alcohol excessively
•Watching TV / using the computer unproductively for long periods of time
•Working obsessively
•Fighting
•Abusing drugs
•Unhealthy eating
