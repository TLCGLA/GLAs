Student Health and Wellness Center
•The Student Health and Wellness Center provides free or low cost, confidential services for students.
•The staff includes licensed clinical psychologists for individual, couples, or group mental health counseling.  Counseling visits are free!  Each student is allowed 6 visits per semester.
•The Center is also an LGBTQ Safe Zone.
