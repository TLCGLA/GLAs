Addressing Test Anxiety
•Here are some strategies to help you address test anxiety:
•Cognitive:  Don’t catastrophize!  Stop yourself from engaging in negative self-talk.
•Emotional:  Leave your baggage at the door.  During a test, leave behind any personal problems you may be facing in order to focus on the exam.
•Behavioral:  Go outside and engage in physical activity.
•Physiological:  Take deep, relaxing breaths.
