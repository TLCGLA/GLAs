Strategy #5:  Sleep
•Lack of sleep can lead us to be irritable and cranky, reducing our ability to deal with the day’s stressors.
•Most adults need 7-9 hours of sleep per night.  Be sure to get adequate sleep so you are well-rested for the day ahead.
•Spend time winding down before going to bed.  Don’t work late into the night or use the phone before bed.  Instead, try reading or listening to music.
•Try to maintain a regular sleep pattern (going to bed and waking up around the same time of day everyday).
•Avoid large meals before bed.
