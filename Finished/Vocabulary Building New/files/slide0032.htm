•If I were beginning to use the word ubiquitous, I would look up the definition first, which is:  being present everywhere.
•
•Then, I would try to think of an example.  For example, in urban and suburban areas Starbucks is ubiquitous.  I can find a Starbucks just about anywhere.
•
•By making personal connections, you have made the word part of your life.  You aren’t going to forget it.
