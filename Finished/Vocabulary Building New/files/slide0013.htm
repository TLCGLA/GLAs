•Sam was committed to showing integrity in his life.  As a child, he refused to let his friends copy his homework.  As an adult, he reported his mistakes to his boss immediately.
•
•To figure out what integrity means, we must look at the examples the author gives us.  How are refusing to let a friend copy and reporting mistakes similar?
•
•The examples are similar because they both relate to honesty.  So, we can guess that “integrity” means “honesty.”
