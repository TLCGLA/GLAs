•Some words are built from different parts.  We can use those parts to help determine a word’s meaning.
•
•
•The prefix “im” means “not”
•The base “percept” means “perceive”
•The suffix “ible” means “able”
•Therefore, “imperceptible” means “unable to be perceived.”
