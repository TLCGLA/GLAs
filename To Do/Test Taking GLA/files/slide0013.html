During The Test...
Work simple to complex, easy to hard.  Go through quickly and answer the questions you know.
Don’t stay too long on any one question.  Circle the harder questions you’ll look at later.  Be careful to skip over their spaces on the answer sheet.
On your second time through, make “educated guesses” if necessary.
Use Time to Your Advantage
(Center for Academic Success; Dunham, 2004; Nugent & Vitale, 2004; UNC School of Nursing)
