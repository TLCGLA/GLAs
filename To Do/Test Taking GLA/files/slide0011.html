Before The Test...
Arrive prepared!
Bring everything you’ll need: pencils, pens, watch, calculator, Scantron sheet, paper, sweater or jacket.
Get there early to choose the seat you like best: lighting, temperature, legroom.
Get there early to gather your thoughts.  Relax!
(Center for Academic Success; Dunham, 2004; Nugent & Vitale, 2004; UNC School of Nursing)
