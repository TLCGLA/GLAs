6.  NUMBER,
¡The source may be part of a numbered sequence; provide that number to help readers locate your source.
¡Examples:
¡Multivolume book.  Indicate the volume number like this:  vol. 2
¡Journals may have volume and issue numbers.  Cite like this:  vol. 12, no. 4
¡Television series have seasons and episodes.  Cite like this:  season 4, episode 10
