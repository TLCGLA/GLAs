OTHER POSSIBILITIES FOR IN-TEXT CITATION
¡If you do not name the author in the signal phrase, include his/her name in the in-text citation.
¡Example:  (Shenk 10).
¡
¡If your work does not include page numbers (like many online sources), do not provide a page number.
¡Example:  If the author is named in a signal phrase
¡Laura Moser describes the possible structure of a four-day school week, stating, Friday attendance will be optional, and instead of regular classes, struggling students can go to tutorials and get more individualized attention from teachers.
¡
¡Example:  If the author is not named in a signal phrase
¡One proposed structure for a four-day school week is, Friday attendance will be optional, and instead of regular classes, struggling students can go to tutorials and get more individualized attention from teachers (Moser).
