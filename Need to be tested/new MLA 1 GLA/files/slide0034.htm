7.  PUBLISHER,
¡The publisher is the organization primarily responsible for producing a source or making it available to the public.
¡Book:  Look on the title or copyright page for the publisher.
¡Film and television series:  Select the company that has primary responsibility for releasing the source.  Often,  Amazon has this information available in Product Details.
¡Websites:  A website may be published by many types of organizations.  Look at the bottom of the page for a copyright notice to find the name of the publisher.
¡Omit the publisher’s name in the following sources:  periodicals (journal / magazine / newspaper), a self-published work, a website whose title is the same as the publisher, a website not involved in producing the works it makes available (like YouTube).  In the case of websites like YouTube, name the site as the container, not the publisher.
