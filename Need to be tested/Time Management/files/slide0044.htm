How do you know if you are procrastinating?
•You might be procrastinating if…
•You spend most of your time on low priority tasks
•You start a high priority tasks and stop almost immediately for a distraction (like getting a cup of coffee)
•You leave an item on your To Do list for a long time, even though you know it’s important.
•You regularly agree to do unimportant tasks that others give you rather than completing your own important tasks.
•You wait for the right mood or the right time to complete a task.
