Overcoming Multitasking Habits
•Schedule tech breaks or other breaks for yourself
•Turn off electronics when working
•Avoid opening multiple tabs on the computer
•Set aside specific times for checking email and other online accounts
•Find methods for avoiding interruptions (like working alone in the library)
•Reset your natural rhythm during the day by taking a short walk outside or opening a window
•When you are interrupted by something important, resist the urge to multitask.  Before switching to a new task, make note of your place in your current task so you can pick it up again easily
