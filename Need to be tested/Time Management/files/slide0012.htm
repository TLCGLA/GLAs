Distinguish Between Long and Short Term Goals
•The goal you create using FOCUS is most likely your long term goal.  It will help you achieve your dreams.
•You should also develop short term goals.  Short term goals are intermediate steps towards achieving your long term goals.
•For example, if your long term goal is to own your own restaurant, you may have to meet some of the following short term goals:
•Take cooking classes to learn how to cook well and safely
•Gain experience working in restaurants or with catering companies
•Network with restaurant owners
•Take business classes to learn how to start and market your own business
