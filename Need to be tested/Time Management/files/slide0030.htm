Sample: 5 Page Research Paper
•In a previous slide, we looked at the different tasks a student may need to complete a research paper.
•You will see the same tasks below.  This time, the student has included deadlines for him/herself.  The final draft of the paper is due on December 8.
•Research and note taking:  Nov. 25
•Draft outline:  Nov. 28
•First draft:  Dec. 1
•Review with a tutor:  Dec. 3
•Make a Works Cited page:  Dec. 3
•Revise paper:  Dec. 4
•Format in MLA style:  Dec. 6
•Proofread paper:  Dec. 6
•Note:  The student plans to finish proofreading on Dec. 6, two days before the paper is due.  S/he has built a buffer just in case s/he runs behind.
