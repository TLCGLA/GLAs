STEP 3:  IN-TEXT CITATION
After the quote, include the page number in parentheses.
This allows your reader to locate your source and learn more.

Example:
According to David Shenk, the whole concept of genetic giftedness turns out to be wildly off the marktragically kept afloat for decades by a cascade of misunderstandings and misleading metaphors (10).

Note the order of the punctuation.
