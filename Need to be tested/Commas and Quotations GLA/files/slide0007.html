Mini-Quiz: Where do the commas belong?
Yoda said, “Do not judge me by my size for my ally is the force.”
My daughter was Princess Leia for Halloween and
my son was Hans Solo.
Margo never saw Star Wars in the theater nor had
she ever rented the DVD.
Studio executives wanted Chewbacca to wear
shorts but George Lucas vetoed the idea.
Using “for” as a conjunction sounds a little old-fashioned, similar to how Yoda talks. Don’t confuse its use as a conjunction with its use as a preposition. (See sentence below: “for Halloween”)
