When Do I Use “an?”
•Use the indefinite article an before nouns that start with vowel sounds (a, e, i, o, u, etc.)
•An iceberg
•An eagle
•An olive
•
•Use an when using a letter of the alphabet that begins with a vowel sound
•Draw an “x” on the paper.
•I’m going to an NAACP-sponsored event.
•
•Use an when using a word that begins with “h,” but the “h” sound is silent
•An hour
•An honest person
