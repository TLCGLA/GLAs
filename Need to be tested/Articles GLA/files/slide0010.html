What Is an Indefinite Article?
•The indefinite articles are “a” and “an.”  They show that the noun can refer to any member of a group.
•
•Let’s look at an example:
•My son wants a car for his eighteenth birthday.
•This sentence refers to any car.  We don’t know which specific car that he wants.
•
•Here’s another example:
•An ant can lift twenty times its own body weight.
•This sentence refers to any ant.  There are many ants in the world, and this sentence could describe any of them.
