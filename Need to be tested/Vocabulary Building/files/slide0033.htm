•After practicing using a word, it becomes part of your life.  However, you need to store the word and use it from time to time.
•
•There are several ways you can store the word:
1.Keep a vocabulary journal.  Enter all the new vocabulary in alphabetical order (using the English alphabet).
2.Make flashcards and practice with them frequently.
3.Set a weekly goal to acquire 5-7 new vocabulary words.
