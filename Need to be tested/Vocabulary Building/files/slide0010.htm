•Writers may include explanations of a word’s meaning. 
•
•Some context markers to show explanations include:  so that, in other words, that is, it means
•
•Sample Sentence :
•Arid regions are characterized by a severe lack of water. 
•
•The writer explains that arid refers to an area that experiences a severe lack of water.  As readers, we can guess that deserts are arid.
