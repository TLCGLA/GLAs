•Sometimes, sentences will include definitions for vocabulary words.  The sentences use context markers to show they are providing a definition.
•
•Some context markers are:  in other words, that is, this means
•
•Sample Sentence:
•Professor Wu gets so excited about discussing history that she sometimes digresses from her lecture notes.  In other words, she strays from her main point.
•
•We may not know what digress means.  But, we can use the context marker “in other words” to find a definition.  From the context marker, we see that digress means straying from the main point.
