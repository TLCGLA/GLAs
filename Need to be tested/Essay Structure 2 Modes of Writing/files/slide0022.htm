Mode 8:  Persuasion
 Use persuasion to convince readers to support a particular idea or argument.  When you use persuasion, you will share your opinion that is supported by strong evidence.
 When using persuasion, you want readers to take action or believe an idea.
 Much of academic writing uses persuasion in some form or another.  Be prepared to use it frequently.
 Persuasive writing relies on logic and supporting evidence.  Good persuasive writers make sure their argument makes sense and is well-supported.
 Persuasive writing may also include a counterargument (discussion of opposing views).
