Sample Persuasion
 “Perhaps nothing is more responsible for the lack of contentment plaguing some Americans today than the outdated notion of the American Dream that has been peddled to all of us for as long as we can remember. I’m referring to the version that usually involves some mention of a white picket fence.
 While USA Today writes, ‘Home ownership is central to the American dream,’ I know of plenty of New Yorkers who might argue that a reasonable rent is. It’s also hard not to wonder if the mortgage meltdown would have happened if so many Americans had not bought into the idea that their American Dream would not be complete without buying a house, specifically a house they could not really afford.”
 Source:  “The American Dream Is Dead, and Good Riddance” by Keli Goff
