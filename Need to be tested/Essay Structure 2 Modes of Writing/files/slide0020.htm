Mode 7:  Process
 Explain the process for completing a task.  What steps must one take?  In what order?
 Consider your purpose.  What do you hope readers will be able to do after completing your process?
 Be detailed enough for other readers to be able to follow the process.
 Assume that your audience is intelligent but inexperienced.
 Do a test run (ask others to follow the steps you outlined).
 You can also write less formal processes where you provide sample steps for meeting certain goals.
