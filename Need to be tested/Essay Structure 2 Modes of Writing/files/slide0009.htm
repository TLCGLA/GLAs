Sample Description
 Tony Montana’s mansion in Scarface is the perfect example of the corrupt American Dream.  The interior is draped in crimson wallpaper; the walls carefully divided into rectangular panels with black lacquer columns and rows.  Within these panels are classical paintings, increasing the overall Roman decadence of the room.  Twin stairways wrap themselves around the side of the room, meeting together at one central balcony.  Their gold filigree bannisters twist and twirl, practically screaming their costly price.  Beneath the balcony is a pool; the center of the pool has a statue of female figures balancing a globe on their heads.  Wrapped around the globe is a neon sign that reads, “the world is yours.”
