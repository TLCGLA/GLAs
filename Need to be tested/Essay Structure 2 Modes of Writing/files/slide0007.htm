Sample Definition
 The American Dream is a cultural belief that argues that people in the United States, from whatever race or class, can succeed through hard work and dedication.  The particulars of the American Dream have changed over time.  For some, “hard work” meant physical labor in difficult conditions; for others, it meant achieving success in education.  Further, the understanding of “success” changes as well.  Some dream of starting their own business and becoming wealthy while others dream of owning a home and providing a better life for their children.
