What you should know about Essay Structure now
 The Introduction / Body / Conclusion format is useful in many communication situations.
 Introductions should ease your readers into your topic and argument.
 Body paragraphs should explain your ideas in detail and support them with evidence.
 Conclusions should remind readers of important ideas and answer the question “so what?”
 As a college student, you should develop your academic discretion to make sure you are communicating effectively.
