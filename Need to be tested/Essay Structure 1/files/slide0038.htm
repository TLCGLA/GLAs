What is the purpose of the conclusion?
 The conclusion is your final word on a topic.  It gives you the opportunity to remind readers about key points in your argument AND emphasize the significance of your argument. 
 The conclusion should answer the question, “so what?”  In other words, tell readers why your topic is important. 
 Audiences remember the last thing they read best.  This means that it is very important to have a strong conclusion so you make a positive, lasting impression on the reader.
