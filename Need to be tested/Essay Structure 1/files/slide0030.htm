Some Types of Body Paragraphs
 Body Paragraphs can fulfill several different functions, including:
◦Thoroughly explaining a reason that supports the writer’s thesis
◦Presenting a counterargument and/or refuting a counterargument
◦Comparing or contrasting ideas
◦Providing relevant background information
◦Explaining a step in a process
◦Analyzing a key example that supports the thesis
