Beyond Formulas
 You may be used to writing within specific formulas for your school assignments.  One of the most common is the Five Paragraph Essay (also known as the Jane Schaffer method).
 These formulas may be helpful for beginning writers.  However, as you progress and your assignments grow more complex (and longer), these formulas are not as effective.
 Eventually, you can start working outside of these formulas. 
 Work on meeting the assignment requirements rather than counting paragraphs and sentences.
