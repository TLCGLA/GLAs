Will I ever need to do this after college is over?
 After college is over, you may or may not find yourself writing academic essays.
 What you will be doing is communicating with other people.  The fundamental essay structure of introduction, body, conclusion is a clear, well-organized way to communicate. 
 You will use intro/body/conclusion when you…
•Write an email
•Make a presentation
•Write a letter of application for a job, a transfer admission, or a scholarship
