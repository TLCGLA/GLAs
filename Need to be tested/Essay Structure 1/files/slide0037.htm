 The correct answer is C:  20% similar.
 The introduction and conclusions should share some ideas—after all, they are from the same paper.  However, the tone of the conclusion is quite different.  Savvy writers use the conclusion to urge action and remind readers why the topic is important. 
 Avoid copying or rewriting the introduction.  Instead, use the conclusion to make a strong final impression on your readers.
