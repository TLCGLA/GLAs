Thesis
 Often, your thesis will appear at the end of the introduction.  This placement gives you a chance to gently introduce readers to your topic before sharing your argument with them.
 The thesis is the main argument you plan to make in your paper.  It is the most important sentence or two in the essay. 
 A good thesis will:
•Answer the question on the essay prompt
•Be debatable
•Be clear
•Be specific
•Provide a preview of the rest of the essay
