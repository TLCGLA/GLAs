Example 1:  Analyzing a Quote Rhetorically
´Quote:  “Man say to his woman:  I got me a dream.  His woman say: Eat your eggs” (Hansberry 1568).
´Analysis:
´Function:  The quote shows that Walter’s frustration doesn’t just apply to his individual situation, but to relationships between men and women in general.
´Placement:  The quote takes place near the beginning of the play, when Walter is arguing with his wife, Ruth.  It gives us insight into Walter’s character and his relationship with his wife.
´Logic:  Walter is pointing out his wife’s distraction technique; he believes she is deliberately avoiding an argument about his liquor store idea.  He also makes a hasty generalization, assuming that his wife’s attitude applies to other marriages.
