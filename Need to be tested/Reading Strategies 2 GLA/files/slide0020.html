Apply
•To apply the information in the text, you should be able to use the material in new situations.  For example, you might apply a rule (like a traffic law) to a particular situation (a driver that is pulled over by a police officer).
•You may ask yourself some of the following questions to show you can apply the information in a text:
•Can I think of an example of the concept in the text?
•How might I use this concept in my education, life, or career?
•In the case of our psychology example, how might I diagnose a patient with social anxiety disorder?
