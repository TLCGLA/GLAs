Syllabus
•
•On the first day of class, you will receive a Syllabus, which is an outline for the class.  The syllabus is an important document that explains exactly what is expected of you over the course of the semester.  Plan to review the syllabus frequently.
•You can expect to see the following components in most syllabi:
•Required Books
•List of Assignments
•Grading Policy
•Attendance/Drop policy
•Academic Integrity Policy
•Office Hours/Location
•Instructor’s Contact Information
•Disability Accommodations
•Student Learning Outcomes
•Assignment/Reading Calendar (this could also be a separate handout)
