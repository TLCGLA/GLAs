Office Hours
•College professors hold office hours.  Office hours are times outside of class when students can meet with their professors one-on-one.
•Use this time to ask individual questions, seek help on an assignment, or discuss personal situations with a professor (like making arrangements to take an exam in TLC instead of the classroom).
•Your professor may want you to make an appointment before visiting office hours.  Be sure to email or speak to him/her after class regarding appointments.
