Student-Centered Learning
•Most professors in the United States will use some aspect of student-centered learning. This means the class might have some or all of the following:
•Debates
•Discussions
•Analyses of problems
•Group work
•Pair work
•It also means that the professor may not always lecture. You should not expect to simply sit and listen.
•You should expect to talk and participate.
•These activities are a vital part of an American education.  They allow you to work with the material, your professor, and your classmates.
•Learning material this way will help you remember and use the material later in life (either on a test, in a paper, or in your career).
