USE A COMMA FOR INTRODUCTORY WORDS
•Sometimes, we use single words to help make the transition from one sentence to the next.  Use a comma after those words.
•Introductory words include:  however, still, furthermore, and meanwhile
•Examples:
•Meanwhile, the police were already tracking the bank robber’s getaway vehicle.
•Furthermore, changing to native, drought-tolerant landscaping may attract birds and butterflies to your garden.
