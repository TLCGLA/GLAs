MYTH BUSTING
•You may have been told to add a comma to a sentence whenever you pause.  This technique is not the most effective. 
•Different people pause in different places when they speak out loud.  Does this mean we all have our own comma rules? 
•Of course not! 
•Instead of trying to find the places you pause, learn the correct rules for comma use.  This will help you properly place a comma every time!
