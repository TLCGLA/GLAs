USE A COMMA TO INTRODUCE QUOTATIONS
•Use a comma between a signal phrase and a quote
•Examples: 
•A famous ad campaign that began in the 1990’s asked, “Got milk?”
•“Brevity is the soul of wit,” declared Polonius.
•
•Don’t use a comma if the quotation is introduced with “that.”
•Example:  The driver the officer pulled over claimed that “the speed limit wasn’t posted anywhere.”
•
•Don’t use a comma if the quote ends with a question mark or explanation point.
•Example:  “We’re getting married!” Bethany squealed with glee.
