Examples of Participle Phrase Fragments
Participle Phrase Fragments
Corrected by Adding a New Independent Clause
Corrected by Adding a Subject and a “To Be” Verb
Watching the clock.
Watching the clock, Bobby waited for his lunch break.
Bobby is watching the clock.
Calling the limo.
Calling the limo, Stacy realized she forgot to bring a tip for the driver.
Stacy is calling the limo.
Bitten by mosquitos.
Bitten by mosquitos, we left our campsite feeling itchy.
The campers were bitten by mosquitos.
