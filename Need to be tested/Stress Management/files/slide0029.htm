If you feel anxious, try:
•Preparing thoroughly for upcoming challenges.  For example, if you are anxious about a test, be sure to study carefully for that test.
•Breathe deeply.  Fear causes the body to constrict; deep breaths will help loosen the body.
•Imagine yourself succeeding at your goals.
•Reframe your thoughts.  Ask yourself, “if the worse happens, can I live with it?”
