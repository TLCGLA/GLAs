Physical Symptoms
•Your body changes when under stress.  You may notice the following physical symptoms:
•Increased heart rate or blood pressure
•Headaches and migraines
•Constant feelings of tiredness
•Nervousness and shaking
•Clenched jaw and grinding teeth
•Tight muscles and joint pain
•Lowered immune system:  frequent colds, sinus infections, and flu symptoms
•Upset stomach, indigestion, heartburn, and ulcers
