Body Mind Wellness Initiative
•The Body Mind Wellness Initiative promotes wellness-related events, groups, and resources.
•These events are usually available on campus, for free.
•Events include the BMW Resource Fair.
•The Fall Fair focuses on challenges to Body Mind Wellness
•The Spring Fair focuses on healthy lifestyle behaviors
•The BMW Initiative also connects students to clubs and support groups like LETS, an organization dedicated to erasing the stigma associated with mental illness.
