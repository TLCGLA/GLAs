What Can You Expect from Your Professor?
•In many countries, professors are respected because they keep a distance from their students.  Students are expected to listen to a professor and speak very little.  The practice of asking questions may be discouraged.
•The same is not necessarily true in American college classrooms.  While some professors are very formal, many prefer a more casual, interactive relationship with their students.
•You can expect to see a variety of teaching techniques, including lectures, class discussions, team activities, PowerPoint presentations, etc.
•Professors will expect you to participate in student-centered learning activities (more information later in the workshop).
•Professors will expect you to ask questions if you do not understand a topic.  In the United States, asking questions is not a sign of disrespect.
