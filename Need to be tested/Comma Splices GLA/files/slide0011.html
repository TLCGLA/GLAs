Correcting Comma Splices
•Comma splices need to be corrected.
•Example:
•College of the Canyons offers many interesting courses, these courses attract students from near and far.
•
•Let’s separate the sentence into two clauses to see if they can stand alone.  In other words, are they independent clauses?
•
•College of the Canyons offers many interesting courses.
•These courses attract students from near and far.
1.
•Each clause is independent.  Therefore, the sentence is a comma splice that must be corrected.
