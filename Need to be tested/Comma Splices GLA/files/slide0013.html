Correct a Comma Splice with a Semicolon (;)
•Let’s use with the same comma splice and correct it using a semicolon.
•Example:
•College of the Canyons offers many interesting courses  ; these courses attract students from near and far.
•
•You may remove the comma and place a semicolon in its place.
•Understand this:
•When you separate two independent clauses with a period (.), the period must be followed by a capital letter.
•
•When you separate  two independent clauses by a semicolon (;), the semicolon must be followed by a lower case letter.
•
