The FYE Application will be open until May 20th at 8:00 pm

To be eligible for the FYE Program for the 2013-14 academic year, a student must:

  • Be a graduating senior from the William S. Hart High School District.
  • Have completed a COC application for fall 2013.
  • Have completed the COC online orientation.
  • Have completed the Math and English Assessment.
  • Based on their assessment results, be placed below 100-level in both their Math and English classes.

For questions regarding English and Math placement, please refer to the COC English and Math placement charts.

The FYE application period is now closed.

After applying, you will receive an email to your my.canyons.edu account confirming receipt of your completed application. Incomplete applications will not be accepted or reviewed.

Students admitted to the FYE Program will be notified via my.canyons.edu email after May 20th, 2013.

For questions, please contact fye@canyons.edu.
