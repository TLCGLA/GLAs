Step One

Exercise: Think Aloud

It's time to practice the "think aloud" technique. Please locate the "Reading Textbooks" worksheet in one of the handouts you were given. Once you have the handouts, watch the video on this page. The video shows two readers thinking out loud while reading. (A transcript [or written copy] of the text is provided for you in one of the handouts if you want to follow along on the text. The handout is called "Reading Textbooks - Transcripts for Think Aloud Models".)
Using the student worksheet, take notes on what the reader says when she thinks aloud. Then refer to the list of strategies at the top of the worksheet. For each model, identify the numbers of three strategies that the reader used and include them in your notes in parts 1 and 2 of the handout. (You will complete part 3 later.) After you are done with this exercise, please click on the "Next" button to the right to continue.
