Time Management Self-Evaluation

Instructions:

How good are you at time management? Evaluate yourself by answering "yes" or "no" to the questions below. As you click on the "yes" or "no" buttons, the counter to the right will show how many points you earned. You will earn or lose points according to how many good time management behaviors you exhibit. If you earn less than 5 points, you need to pick up a few more time management skills. If you earn close to the full 10 points, you have strong time management skills. After you earn at least five points, a button will appear on the top right corner. Click on the button to continue. Note: You can restart the exercise as many times as you want by reloading the browser page.
