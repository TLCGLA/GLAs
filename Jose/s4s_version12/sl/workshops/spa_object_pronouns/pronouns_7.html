Questions With Indirect Object Pronouns
Instructions: Formulate questions and answers with the clues given, then practice asking and answering the questions with a partner.

1.
Do you bring me chocolates?
(Yes, I bring you chocolates every day.)
2.
Can you give her your notes?
(Yes, I always give her my notes.)
3.
Are you going to buy us a Coke?
(No, you are going to buy me a Coke!)
4.
Do you have to read them the letter (carta)?
(No, I don't have to read them the letter.)
5.
Does the professor give you (all) many exams?
(Yes! she gives us exams every day.)
6.
Is she going to ask us for money (dinero)?
(No, she is asking him for money today.)
7.
Is your grandmother buying you a car?
(Yes, she is buying me an old car.)
8.
Do you cook me dinner?
(No, I don't want to cook you dinner.)
9.
Can you sing me "la cucaracha"?
(Yes, I can sing you "la cucaracha".)
10.
Are you going to buy us a pizza?
(Yes! I am going to buy you (all) a big pizza.)
