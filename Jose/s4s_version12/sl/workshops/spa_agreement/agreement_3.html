Agreement
In English the words "a" and "some" are called articles. The word "the", as in "the book" is also an article.

The difference between "a" and "some" and "the" is that "a" and "some" are indefinite articles, while "the" is a definite article.

In Spanish there are also definite and indefinite articles:
Definite Articles
the book   =
el libro
the books     =
los libros
the house =
la casa
the houses   =
las casas
 
Indefinite Articles
a book     =
un libro
some books   =
unos libros
a house   =
una casa
some houses =
unas casas

Note that there are masculine and feminine forms for each kind of article. Articles must agree! Learn more.
