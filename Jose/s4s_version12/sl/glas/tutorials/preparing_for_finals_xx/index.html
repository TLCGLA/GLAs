Preparing for Finals
Introduction

The weeks before finals week are often “crunch time” for many students. You not only feel overwhelmed with deadlines for papers, presentations, and projects, but you also need to study for finals. In addition, you may have additional work or family responsibilities. This activity will help you “pull it together” for finals week by proper planning and strategizing.

Student Learning Outcomes

By the end of this activity, you should be able to:

  • Evaluate your needs
  • Prioritize a “to-do” list
  • Create an effective study plan
  • Identify sources of information and support
