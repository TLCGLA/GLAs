Percent and Interest
Introduction

Anywhere you find money, sooner or later, you will come across the concepts of percents and interest. For example, a credit card will charge you a percent, a store may give you a discount based on a percent of the price, and a business owner will mark up his goods to make a profit. Understanding these concepts will help you immensely to make informed money decisions in your life.

Student Learning Outcomes

By the end of this activity, you should be able to:

  • Set up and solve various kinds of percent problems including discount, mark up, and sales tax
