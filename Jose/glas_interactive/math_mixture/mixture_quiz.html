Mixture Word Problems Quiz
1.
How many pounds of dried fruit costing $1.50 per pound should be mixed with 12 pounds of granola that costs $2.00 per pound in order to make a mixture that costs $1.80 per pound?
2.
A chemist needs to mix some 3% acid solution with some 11% acid solution in order to make a total of 50 mL of 6% acid solution. How much 3% solution and 10% solution should she mix?
3.
How much pure water must be mixed with 20 Liters of 10% alcohol solution in order to make a mixture that is 4% alcohol? (Hint: pure water has 0% alcohol.)
