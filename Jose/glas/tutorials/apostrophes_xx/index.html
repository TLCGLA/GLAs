Apostrophes
Introduction

The apostrophe is one of the most frequently misunderstood and misused punctuation marks. As a college student, you will want to make sure that your essays, exams, and emails to your professor do not have errors. Understanding when to use the apostrophe, as well as when NOT to use it, is one important step you can take to help create impressive papers that represent you well.

Student Learning Outcomes

By the end of this activity, you should be able to:

  • Know when to use the apostrophe
  • Know when not to use the apostrophe
