Time Management
Introduction

Time management is an important skill for you to master, as it can affect everything you do in and out of college. Without proper time management, you will find yourself continously stressed, unable to finish projects as well as you expected, and frustrated by lower grades. Time management is not easy for any of us: It requires making difficult choices and sticking to them in a world that keeps adding demands on our personal and professional lives.

Student Learning Outcomes

By the end of this activity, you should be able to:

  • Evaluate and create a student schedule
  • Indicate three ways to use time more effectively
